How does Bitcoin work?

Unlike traditional currencies, Bitcoin is not issued or maintained by a centralized government entity. Instead, a decentralized network of computer nodes is responsible for maintaining Bitcoin. This decentralization means the holders and users of Bitcoin can help to maintain the network.
